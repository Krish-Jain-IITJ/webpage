About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. My research focuses on theoretical and applied aspects of algorithms, combinatorics, graph theory, and parameterized complexity.

I am particularly interested in designing efficient algorithms for hard combinatorial problems, fixed-parameter tractability, and their connections to structural graph theory. My work spans both the theoretical foundations and practical implications of algorithmic problem solving.

Prior to joining IIT Jodhpur, I was a postdoctoral researcher at leading research institutions. I completed my PhD from IIT Delhi, where I developed a deep interest in exact and parameterized algorithms for NP-hard problems.

I warmly invite prospective PhD students with a strong interest in algorithms, graph theory, or parameterized complexity to reach out at anoopjain@iitj.ac.in.
